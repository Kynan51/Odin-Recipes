Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour (large)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoon Margarine
  • Cooking oil or cooking fat

procedure

  1. Mix the flour with the margarine salt and sugar
  2. Gradually add water untill your dough is well mixed and not hard and not runny,,,,then add oil while neading the dough leave to sit for about 15 minuites
  3. Make the dough into balls depending on the size you want
  4. Roll the balls each and add oil then roll and place aside
  5. After done leave to sit for about 20 mins
  6. Roll the dough and put on the pan leave it untill it cooks on both sides then add oil and leave it to cook till golden brown
  7. When ready searvd as desired mine I ate with,,,,rice and chicken stew
